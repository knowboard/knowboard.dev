---
title: What is Knowboard?
---

Knowboard turns Markdown files into a rich [Personal Knowledge Base](
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Personal_knowledge_base).

Use it for anything like keeping track of your favorite movies, building
personal productivity workflows, or tracking your health and fitness routines.

Your data is stored locally so only you have access to it.

## Who is this for?

Knowboard's current tooling is aimed specifically at power users. It's for
people who like working in a code editor and want powerful
[querying](/guides/querying) functionality.

Future iterations will aim to ease the learning curve, but for now it does
require a desire to understand some of the technical details.
